# Dream Drive Motors: Digital Presence Handoff Document ## 1. Project Overview Dream Drive Motors is a premium, concierge-level car leasing dealership. The digital experience is designed to be "pristine and elite," emphasizing personalized service, high-end aesthetics, and a "Budget-First" approach to luxury car acquisition. ## 2. Design System: Dream Drive Elite This document follows the `{{DATA:DESIGN_SYSTEM:DESIGN_SYSTEM_1}}` design system. ### Visual Identity * **Primary Color:** Champagne Gold (#D4AF37) - Used for primary actions, accents, and highlighting key financial data. * **Secondary Color:** Deep Charcoal (#131313) - The base for the "Dark Mode" aesthetic. * **Typography:** * **Serif (Newsreader):** Used for headlines and prominent titles to convey a sense of heritage and luxury. * **Sans-Serif (Manrope):** Used for technical data, labels, and body text for high readability and a modern, professional feel. * **Spacing & Layout:** Generous white space (breathable layouts), editorial-style grids, and "glassmorphism" effects for depth. --- ## 3.
