Animation & Interaction Level

How cinematic should the DDM experience feel? Pick the tier that matches your vision.

A

Refined & Subtle

Scroll-triggered fade-ins, smooth page transitions, hover states on cards/buttons.

Elements fade and slide in as you scroll. Vehicle cards scale on hover. Navigation has smooth underline animations. Page transitions crossfade. Think: Apple.com product pages.

Pros

  • Fast to build
  • Performant on all devices
  • Timeless, never feels "gimmicky"

Cons

  • Less "wow factor" on first visit
B

Cinematic & Immersive

Everything in A, plus: parallax hero images, staggered reveal sequences, number counters, scroll-linked progress, image reveal animations.

Hero images have parallax depth. Stats animate/count up when scrolled into view. Service cards reveal with staggered delays. Vehicle gallery images have clip-path reveal effects. Sections have scroll-triggered storytelling sequences. Think: Porsche.com or Rolls-Royce configurator.

Pros

  • Strong "wow" factor
  • Matches luxury brand positioning
  • Great storytelling through motion

Cons

  • More build time
  • Needs performance tuning on mobile
C

Full Showroom Experience

Everything in B, plus: custom cursor, smooth scroll (Lenis), magnetic buttons, text split animations, scroll-hijacked hero sequences, GSAP-powered timeline animations.

Custom gold cursor that reacts to interactive elements. Smooth locomotive-style scrolling. Headlines split and animate letter-by-letter. Hero sections "pin" and animate through states as you scroll. Magnetic hover effects on buttons. Think: Award-winning agency sites (Awwwards).

Pros

  • Maximum impact
  • Award-worthy experience
  • Unforgettable first impression

Cons

  • Significantly more build time
  • Can hurt mobile UX if not careful
  • Accessibility concerns with scroll hijacking

My recommendation: B (Cinematic & Immersive) — it delivers the luxury feel DDM needs without the UX risks of scroll hijacking. We'd use Framer Motion for scroll animations and GSAP for the lease calculator and number counters.
